# Consumer EEG

### 2025-01-27

Since EEG (electroencephalography) is a low-cost and safe neuroimaging technique, I have compiled here a list of consumer EEG devices including both packaged products and do-it-yourself kits.

#### All-In-One Consumer

- [EMOTIV](https://www.emotiv.com/)
- [InteraXon Muse](https://choosemuse.com/)
- [Neurable MW75 Neuro](https://www.neurable.com/products/mw75neuro)
- [Neurable MW75 Neuro LT](https://www.neurable.com/products/adhd)
- [NeuroSky MindWave](https://neurosky.com/)
- [Neurosity Crown](https://neurosity.co/tech-specs)
- [Sens.ai](https://sens.ai/store/headset)

#### Sleep / Wellness EEG

- [FocusCalm](https://focuscalm.com/products/focuscalm-eeg-headband)
- [FRENZ Brainband](https://frenzband.com/products/frenz-brainband)

#### Prosumer / Research Wearables

- [Brain Products LiveAmp](https://www.brainproducts.com/solutions/liveamp/)
- [Cognionics / CGX](https://www.cgxsystems.com/technology)
- [g.tec Unicorn Hybrid Black](https://www.gtec.at/product/unicorn-hybrid-black/)
